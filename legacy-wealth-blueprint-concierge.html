Why Most Rentals Do Not Change A High Earner's Tax Picture

Most people hear "rental depreciation" and assume any property will help their W-2 taxes. That is not how it works.

Most long-term rentals are treated as passive, which means the paper losses usually do not help your W-2 income. That is why so many high earners buy rentals and still do not get the tax outcome they expected.

Short-term rentals can be different. If the stay length and participation rules are handled correctly, the depreciation may be able to offset active income without needing Real Estate Professional Status. That is the mechanism people get excited about.
