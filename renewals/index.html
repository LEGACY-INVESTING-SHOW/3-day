FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to decide which option is right for you.

What exactly am I renewing?
The implementation layer -- your 12-Month Wealth Plan, community access, weekly calls, AI access, and your Wealth Guide. The course gives you the strategies; this is what helps you implement them. It runs on a 12-month cycle. The video course is yours for life and doesn't need to be renewed.
What are Wealth Guides?
Wealth Guides are your main point of contact inside the program. They can get on calls with you whenever you need, answer your questions, give you accountability, and help you implement the strategies you're learning. Think of them as your personal guide through the wealth-building process.
What is the 12-Month Wealth Plan?
It's a 12-month roadmap tailored to your income, goals, and timeline that tells you exactly what financial moves to make and when. Your current plan expires with your implementation access. A new 12-Month Wealth Plan comes with Option 2 and Option 3, and includes a 1-on-1 call with Coach Jeff.
Who should choose the Community + Calls option?
Option 1 is for you if your strategy feels solid and you want the implementation support to keep executing -- the weekly calls, your Wealth Guide, AI access, and a community of people who understand what you're building.
Who should choose the 12-Month Wealth Plan?
Option 2 is for you if your situation has changed since you first joined -- or if the tax landscape has. Maybe you implemented strategies from your first plan and your financial picture looks different now. Maybe your income jumped, your goals evolved, or your family or business situation changed. On top of that, the tax code itself has changed -- there are new rules, new thresholds, and new strategies available in 2026 that didn't exist when your first plan was built. This gives you a new 12-Month Wealth Plan based on where you actually are today and the latest tax landscape, plus a call with Coach Jeff to map out your next moves.
What happens on the call with Coach Jeff?
You'll walk through your new 12-Month Wealth Plan together, talk about what's changed in your life and finances, and get clear next steps based on your current goals. It's a strategy session tailored to where you are right now.
Can I start with one option and upgrade later?
Yes. If you start with Option 1 or Option 2 and later want the Full Renewal Bundle, you can upgrade within 30 days and we'll credit what you already paid toward Option 3.
What happens if I don't renew?
Your 12-Month Wealth Plan, community access, weekly calls, AI access, and Wealth Guide access will end. You'll keep lifetime access to the full video course -- that never goes away. If you want to rejoin the implementation support later, you can always reach out.
