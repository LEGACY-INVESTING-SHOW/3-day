No. The system has worked for W-2 employees, self-employed people, business owners, couples, and singles. The issue is not your label. The issue is whether money is being left on the table right now.

The team backs this with a results guarantee: if you put in the work and do not see a full return, they keep working with you for free until you do. That guarantee only makes sense if the math is measurable and the system is repeatable.

No. The Bridge Method is an option for clients who showed interest and have the right foundation in place. Some clients add it. Others focus entirely on the wealth plan. The team helps you decide what fits.

"Can I do the Airbnb part with a full-time job?"

Yes. The Bridge Method was built for busy people. The goal is to get the first unit into a low-hour weekly rhythm once the systems and automations are set up.
